HomePulse Support

What is HomePulse?

HomePulse is a diagnostic tool for HomeKit and Matter devices that provides real-time monitoring, battery tracking, and network analysis.

Why can't I see all my devices?

Ensure HomePulse has HomeKit access in Settings > Privacy & Security > HomeKit. Some devices may take time to appear.

How does the Health Score work?

The Health Score analyzes device connectivity, battery levels, and recent events to provide an overall home health rating.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data stays on your device and iCloud. Matter codes are encrypted before storage.
